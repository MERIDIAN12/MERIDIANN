Bathroom Repipe $14,200

Forest Hill Ensuite Repipe

Forest Hill, ON · 6 days

Budget$14,200
Timeline6 working days
ScopeFull ensuite repipe, double vanity

The problem: Original 1962 galvanized supply lines were corroding from the inside, causing visibly rusty water and falling water pressure throughout the upper floor.

What we did: Replaced the full supply run with copper, added a double-vanity manifold, and relocated the shower valve to accommodate a larger glass enclosure being installed by the client's contractor.

Kitchen Reno Plumbing $22,800

Leslieville Kitchen & Powder Room

Leslieville, ON · 9 days

Budget$22,800
Timeline9 working days
ScopeIsland sink, gas range, powder room

The problem: Client wanted an island sink and a gas range, neither of which the existing rough-in supported, plus a new powder room off the mudroom that had no plumbing at all.

What we did: Ran new supply and drain lines under the slab to the island, installed a dedicated gas line with shutoff for the range, and fully rough-in plumbed the new powder room from scratch.

Slab cutting was sequenced with the GC so it happened in a single pass, no return trips.

Rosedale, ON · 3 weeks, phased

Budget$31,500
Timeline3 weeks, phased
ScopeFull home, polybutylene to copper

Backwater Valve & Drainage

Oakville Backwater Valve Install

Oakville, ON · 2 days

Budget$4,600
Timeline2 working days
ScopeCity-mandated backwater valve, sump tie-in

The problem: Basement flooded twice in two years during heavy storms, and the city's backflow prevention bylaw required a certified valve before any insurance claim would be honoured again.

What we did: Installed a code-compliant backwater valve, tied it into the existing sump system, and filed the documentation needed for the city subsidy program.

Client recovered part of the cost through Toronto's basement flooding subsidy, which we helped them apply for.
